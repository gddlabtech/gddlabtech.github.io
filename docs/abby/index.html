Abbotsford Studio Guide

C1420#

C1420 is the main studio for GDD on the Abbotsford Campus. This new lab brings new responsibilities that faculty, students, and staff all need to share. One of the ways that we want to do this is to utilize it with some buffer "Shop time" like you might have had in high school woodworking. Where, at the end of each class, we all pitch in together to sanitize and store laptops, papers, knives, cutting mats, wipe down tables, etc. Basically, getting it ready for the class coming in here right after you to use it. Rearranging the tables is the responsibility of the start of the class coming in next. Faculty and Instructors, please note to leave some buffer time in your lesson plans for the start and end of each section for time spent in this classroom.

Projector and Pods#

The podium can broadcast to the 4 screens and projector simultaneously, or the 4 pods can connect individually to their particular screen. Under each TV are the wall plates that allow you to turn On and Off the TV. They also allow you to switch input from the HDMI port at each pod or from the instructor's podium. There are also wall power outlets for your laptop; additional chargers and power bars are located in the storage above the laptop cart. Here you will also find the HDMI cords that you will need to plug in your laptop via USB-C to the wall port at each pod.

In addition to the pods, we also have a brand-new projector system in here that can be controlled or take the video input from the instructor station up at the front with the podium, or from the Apple TV in the podium itself now the system that controls this is to be used only by faculty and instructors arm and if you are a student that is going to be presenting from up here you will need to have the instructor set it up for you first. That being said, to get the projector up and to work, whether you want to output to all the screens in the room or just to the projector, you can choose which one you want to do here. When you walk up to the Extron display on the podium, there are two buttons to turn on the system one says "present using projector only." The other one says, "present using projector and pod displays." You have four display input options once you decide which display output you wish to use from the podium. One of them is PC output. There is a Windows PC computer built into the podium if there are no other computers available. Next, there is HDMI one, which is for a laptop with just HDMI; there's USB-C for MacBooks, or you can choose to output from the Apple TV.

If you are a student sitting at a pod and want to show something on your screen to all of your fellow classmates. Get the instructor to select Apple TV as the video output to display on all screens. From your own MacBook, choose from the screen mirroring options to airplay to C1420 Apple TV. This will allow you to share your screen on all the screens in the room and the projector. If you are just wanting to share your screen on your own individual pod, this is where are you where you would use the HDMI port with the cables that are stored in the cabinet.

C1420 Laptops#

You are welcome to use your own laptop, but if you do not have access to one, we have 20 available for your use. These are all stored in the cart at the back of the classroom. At the start of each semester, we will assign you your own specific laptop; you will be able to find this list around the room.

The laptop that you are assigned is your responsibility. You are personally responsible for getting it plugged in at the end of your course and being sanitized and put away. Note that none of these laptops may leave C1420 for any reason! Even if you were printing down the hall in the printer in C1106 must print from C1420 and then walk back to get your parts must not bring the laptop with you. This is very important.

If you do not have a laptop assigned, please wait until everyone else takes a computer and then take one that's not in use, but please let the GDD technicians know which one you took.

At the end of each class, please make sure that you plug the laptop back into the cart. These laptops in the cart have a specific orientation to all be plugged in, so please note the sticker on top of the computer as to which way the laptop needs to face out to plug into the charger.

C1420 has a unique wifi only accessible in the room called GDDWiFi. The password is: GDDwifi2019

note

Servers#

To connect to the Server from C1420 you must be connected to the GDD WiFi. Please refer to our File Server Access page for instructions on how to connect.

Printers#

The printer will contribute to your semester printer use limit of 75 prints for each GDD course that you were taking. Do Not use this or any GDD printer for printing non-GDD courses or personal material.

Printer in C1420 - Xerox Versalink#

important

If you do not have the printer driver contact ggdlabtech@ufv.ca as soon as possible

To print using the Xerox C7030:

  • Click File -> print or press ⌘ + P on your keyboard to bring up the print menu.
  • Select C1420 Versalink from the printer list
  • Click Print

To access the printers special features

  • Click File -> print or press ⌘ + P on your keyboard to bring up the print menu.
  • Select C1420 Versalink from the printer list
  • Click Copies & Pages and select Xerox Features
  • Navigate the menu and select your Feature
  • Click Print

Printer in C1106 - Xerox 550#

  1. After printing a PDF file from your Mac to the Printer, your file will be in the HELD cue.
  2. If the Printer is sleeping, wake it up by pressing the Green Light (Energy Saver button)
  3. Press the “Fiery” icon on the touchscreen. Touch the Jobs tab at the top, then touch “Held” tab to bring up the list of jobs in the queue.
  4. Once you see the jobs, touch “Login” at the bottom of the screen.
  5. On the Login popup, select “Operator”, no password, then touch “Login”.
  6. Two icons should appear next to each job, a small “Print” icon and “Trash” icon. Touch “Print” to print your job, or “Trash” to delete it.
  7. Choose the number of copies and touch “Print”. Your print should come out on on the right end of the Printer.

C1120 Mac Lab#

C1120 is the main MacLab. This room has 25 iMacs, plus an instructor iMac, and an Extron projector. Drop in times are posted on the MacLab door. Please ask your instructor for the door codes.

WiFi#

C1420 has a unique wifi only accessible in the room called MacLabWifi. The password is: internet

note

Please ensure you are on Mac Lab Wifi before you attempt to print or connect to our servers.

C1122 Homework Studio#

Room C1122 is the smaller of the 2 Maclabs on the Abbotsford Campus.

It consists of 8 iMacs installed with Adobe CC, and UTC. you will also have access to a black and white printer, and a photo scanner.

Please ask your instructor for the door code.

note

The Mac Labs will be available for drop in use from 7:00 AM - 10:00 Weekdays, and 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM on Saturdays.

The Mac Labs are closed on Sundays.

important

No food or drink is allowed in the MacLabs
